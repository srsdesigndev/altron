Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really secure?

Absolutely. Your passwords are encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption and stored locally on your device. Your master key never leaves your device, and we have no access to your data.

What if I forget my master key?

Unfortunately, if you forget your master key, your passwords cannot be recovered. This is by design - it ensures that only you can access your passwords. Make sure to choose a memorable master key!

Do I need internet to use this?

No! The app works completely offline. Once you open it in your browser, you can generate and manage passwords without any internet connection.

Which browsers are supported?

The app requires Chrome or Edge browser for the File System Access API. These browsers allow the app to save encrypted files directly to your computer.

Is this really free?

Yes, 100% free and open source. No hidden costs, no subscriptions, no premium features. The entire source code is available for you to inspect and modify.

Can I use this on mobile?

The interface is responsive and works on mobile browsers, but the File System Access API has limited support on mobile. We recommend using it on desktop for the best experience.
